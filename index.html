Featured Projects

MPC + Residual Reinforcement Learning for Quadrupedal Robots

A hybrid locomotion framework combining convex model predictive control with a Soft Actor-Critic residual policy for the Unitree A1 quadruped in MuJoCo.

The learned policy outputs foothold corrections and joint torque residuals to augment the analytical controller on rough terrain.
